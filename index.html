Welcome

Passionate engineer, researcher, and educator with a strong focus on bio-instrumentation, assistive technologies in neuro-rehabilitation, and medical signal & image processing. With interdisciplinary expertise spanning electronics & instrumentation engineering, biomedical engineering, healthcare technology, and clinical research, I bring an application-oriented and analytical approach to designing and validating technology-driven healthcare solutions. My work emphasizes the integration of electronics and instrumentation techniques to address real-world clinical challenges. I am driven to bridge engineering and healthcare through teaching and translational research, working collaboratively in multidisciplinary environments to transform innovations into meaningful, patient-centred outcomes.
